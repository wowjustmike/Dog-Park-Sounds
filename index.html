Fort Greene Park

Fort Greene Park is a 30.2-acre park in Brooklyn, New York City. It was named after the fort which was formerly located there, originally named Fort Putnam, and then renamed in 1812 for Nathanael Greene, a hero of the American Revolutionary War.
